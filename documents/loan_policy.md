# Loan Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

SecureBank offers a range of personal and secured loan products. Please review eligibility requirements, rates, and terms below.

---

## Personal Loans

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Loan amounts | $1,000 – $50,000 |
| APR range | 6.99% – 24.99% (based on creditworthiness) |
| Term length | 12 – 60 months |
| Origination fee | 1% – 6% of loan amount |
| Prepayment penalty | None |

### Eligibility

- Must be **18 years or older**
- Must have a **credit score of 620 or higher**
- Must provide **proof of income** (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
- Must be a **U.S. citizen or permanent resident**
- Debt-to-income ratio must be below **40%**

---

## Auto Loans

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| New vehicle APR | 4.49% – 12.99% |
| Used vehicle APR | 5.49% – 14.99% |
| Loan amounts | $5,000 – $100,000 |
| Term length | 24 – 72 months |
| Down payment | Minimum **10%** recommended |

### Requirements

- Vehicle must be no older than **10 years** at loan maturity
- Maximum mileage for used vehicles: **100,000 miles** at time of purchase
- Full collision and comprehensive **insurance required**
- Vehicle serves as **collateral** for the loan

---

## Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Credit line | $10,000 – $500,000 |
| APR | Prime rate + 0.5% to Prime + 5.0% (variable) |
| Draw period | 10 years |
| Repayment period | 20 years |
| Annual fee | $50 (waived first year) |

### Requirements

- Must own a primary residence with at least **20% equity**
- Credit score of **680 or higher**
- Property appraisal required (bank covers cost for lines over $100,000)

---

## Application Process

1. **Apply online** at securebank.com/loans or visit a branch
2. Receive a **decision within 1–2 business days**
3. If approved, review and **sign loan documents** (electronic signature accepted)
4. Funds disbursed within **3–5 business days** of signing
5. First payment due **30 days** after disbursement

---

## Late Payment Policy

- A **grace period of 15 days** is provided after each due date
- Late fee: **$39** or **5% of the payment amount**, whichever is less
- Payments more than **30 days late** are reported to credit bureaus
- Payments more than **60 days late** may result in **default proceedings**

---

## Hardship Programs

If you're experiencing financial difficulty:

- Contact us **before** missing a payment
- We offer **temporary payment reduction** (up to 6 months)
- **Deferment options** available for qualifying circumstances (job loss, medical emergency)
- Call our hardship team at **1-800-555-0199, option 3**
