# Transfer Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

SecureBank provides multiple ways to move money. Review transfer options, limits, and processing times below.

---

## Internal Transfers (Between SecureBank Accounts)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost | Free |
| Processing time | **Instant** |
| Daily limit | **$50,000** |
| Scheduling | Immediate, scheduled, or recurring |

---

## External Transfers (ACH)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost | Free |
| Processing time | **1–3 business days** |
| Daily limit | **$10,000** (can be increased to $25,000 upon request) |
| Monthly limit | **$50,000** |

### Requirements

- External account must be **verified** via micro-deposits (1–2 business days)
- Both accounts must be in the **same name** (or joint accounts)
- First-time external transfers may be held for **additional verification**

---

## Wire Transfers

### Domestic Wire Transfers

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost (outgoing) | **$25** |
| Cost (incoming) | **Free** |
| Processing time | **Same business day** if submitted before **4 PM EST** |
| Daily limit | **$100,000** |

### International Wire Transfers

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost (outgoing) | **$45** |
| Cost (incoming) | **$15** |
| Processing time | **2–5 business days** |
| Daily limit | **$50,000** |
| Exchange rate | Mid-market rate + **1.5% margin** |

### Wire Transfer Requirements

- Must provide recipient's **full bank name, routing/SWIFT number, and account number**
- International wires require **recipient's full address** and **bank's SWIFT/BIC code**
- Wires over **$10,000** require **additional identity verification** (federal reporting requirement)
- Wire transfers **cannot be reversed** once sent — verify all details carefully

## Peer-to-Peer Payments (SecurePay)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost | Free |
| Processing time | **Instant** (to other SecureBank customers) |
| External (to non-customers) | **1–3 business days** |
| Daily limit | **$2,500** |
| Weekly limit | **$5,000** |

### Requirements

- Recipient must have a **valid email address or mobile number** on file
- Sender must have **sufficient available balance** (no overdraft for P2P)
- Transactions over **$1,000** require **two-factor authentication**

---

## Bill Pay

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cost | Free |
| Electronic payments | **1–2 business days** |
| Check payments | **5–7 business days** |
| Scheduling | One-time, scheduled, or recurring |

---

## Transfer Cancellation

- **Internal transfers:** Cannot be cancelled once completed (instant)
- **ACH transfers:** Can be cancelled within **30 minutes** of submission if not yet processed
- **Wire transfers:** Cannot be reversed — contact us immediately if sent in error; recall requests are **not guaranteed** and may take **2–4 weeks**
- **SecurePay:** Can be cancelled if recipient has **not yet accepted** the payment

---

## Security Measures

All transfers are protected by:

- **256-bit SSL encryption**
- **Two-factor authentication** for transactions over specified thresholds
- **Velocity checks** — unusual transfer patterns trigger manual review
- **OFAC screening** on all outbound wire transfers
