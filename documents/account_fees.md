# Account Fees & Pricing

**Last updated: January 2025**

Welcome to SecureBank. Below is a summary of our account types, associated fees, and account management details.

---

## Account Types

| Account Type | Monthly Fee | Minimum Balance | Interest Rate (APY) |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Basic Checking | $0 | None | 0.01% |
| Premium Checking | $12.99/month | $1,500 | 0.15% |
| Savings Account | $0 | $100 | 0.50% |
| High-Yield Savings | $0 | $5,000 | 4.25% |
| Student Checking | $0 | None | 0.01% |

### Fee Waivers

- **Premium Checking** monthly fee is waived if daily balance stays above **$1,500** or if you have a direct deposit of **$500+** per month
- **Savings Account** incurs a **$5/month** fee if balance drops below **$100**

---

## Overdraft Fees

- Overdraft fee: **$35 per transaction**
- Maximum overdraft fees per day: **3 transactions ($105 maximum)**
- Overdraft protection transfer fee: **$12** per transfer from linked savings
- We will **not** charge an overdraft fee if your account is overdrawn by **$5 or less**

### Overdraft Protection Options

1. **Link a savings account** — automatic transfer ($12 fee per transfer)
2. **Overdraft line of credit** — interest-based, no per-transaction fee (subject to credit approval)
3. **Decline all overdraft transactions** — opt out entirely at no cost

---

## ATM Fees

- **SecureBank ATMs:** Free and unlimited
- **Partner network ATMs:** Free (over 30,000 locations nationwide)
- **Out-of-network ATMs:** $3.00 per transaction (the ATM owner may charge an additional fee)
- **International ATMs:** $5.00 per transaction plus **3% foreign transaction fee**

## How to Close an Account

1. Ensure your balance is **$0** or transfer remaining funds
2. Contact us at **support@securebank.com** or call **1-800-555-0199**
3. Visit any **SecureBank branch** with a valid photo ID
4. Account closure is processed within **1–2 business days**
5. Any remaining balance will be mailed as a **cashier's check** within **5–7 business days**
