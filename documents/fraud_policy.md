# Fraud Policy & Dispute Resolution

**Last updated: January 2025**

SecureBank is committed to protecting your accounts. This policy covers fraud detection, reporting procedures, and your rights as an account holder.

---

## Fraud Detection

Our systems monitor for suspicious activity **24/7**, including:

- Transactions in unusual locations
- Multiple rapid transactions
- Purchases that differ significantly from your spending patterns
- Card-not-present transactions with mismatched billing information

### Automatic Protections

- **Zero liability** for unauthorized transactions reported within **60 days**
- Real-time transaction alerts via SMS and email (must be enabled in account settings)
- Automatic card freeze if suspicious activity is detected
- Two-factor authentication on all online transactions over **$500**

---

## Reporting Fraud

If you suspect unauthorized activity on your account:

1. **Immediately** call our fraud hotline: **1-800-555-0199, option 1** (available 24/7)
2. Log in to your account and **freeze your card** via the Security section
3. File a **written dispute** within **60 days** of the statement date showing the unauthorized transaction
4. File a **police report** for transactions over **$500** (we may request a copy)

### What to Report

- Date and amount of each unauthorized transaction
- Whether your card was lost, stolen, or still in your possession
- When you first noticed the unauthorized activity
- Any suspects or circumstances you're aware of

---

## Dispute Resolution Timeline

| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Acknowledge your dispute | Within **1 business day** |
| Issue provisional credit | Within **10 business days** |
| Complete investigation | Within **45 days** (90 days for international or new accounts) |
| Final resolution notification | Within **3 business days** of completing investigation |

### Provisional Credit

- For disputes involving **$100 or more**, a provisional credit is issued within **10 business days** while we investigate
- If the investigation finds the transaction was authorized, the provisional credit will be reversed with **5 business days' notice**

---

## Your Liability

| Scenario | Your Maximum Liability |
|----------|----------------------|
| Report before unauthorized use | **$0** |
| Report within **2 business days** of learning of loss/theft | **$50** |
| Report after 2 business days but within **60 days** of statement | **$500** |
| Report after **60 days** of statement | **Unlimited** (for transactions after the 60-day period) |

---

## Identity Theft

If you believe you are a victim of identity theft:

1. Place a **fraud alert** on your credit reports (Experian, Equifax, TransUnion)
2. Contact us to **close compromised accounts** and open new ones
3. File an **Identity Theft Report** at identitytheft.gov
4. We will waive all fees associated with account closure and reopening
5. Free **credit monitoring** for **12 months** provided upon request
